# Workforce Re-entry Navigator — Staff Runbook

## Daily Operation (No Engineering Required)

### Starting the App
1. Open the web tool URL (provided by your tech lead)
2. Check the sidebar shows "✅ API Connected"
3. If not, contact tech lead — do NOT try to fix yourself

### Processing a New Client
1. Click **📝 New Client Intake** in the sidebar
2. Fill out ALL fields (the more detail, the better matches)
3. Click **🚀 Submit & Process**
4. Wait 30-60 seconds for AI processing
5. Review results in three sections:
   - **Client Assessment** — strengths, readiness, recommended support
   - **Job Matches** — scored opportunities with explanations
   - **Cover Letter Draft** — ready to customize

### Interpreting Match Scores

| Score | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 80-100% | **Strong match** — present to client first |
| 65-79% | **Good match** — viable with some support |
| 50-64% | **Marginal** — discuss barriers with client |
| <50% | Not shown — below threshold |

### Downloading Cover Letters
- Click **⬇️ Download as .txt** in the Cover Letter section
- Open in Word/Google Docs to customize
- **Always review before sending** — add specific details only you know

### When to Override the AI
- You know the employer personally and they're more flexible than the database shows
- Client has a hidden strength not captured in intake
- The "plain English" explanation doesn't match what you know about the client

**How to override:** Simply present a different match to the client — the AI is advisory, you decide.

## Monthly Maintenance

### Adding New Jobs
Contact your tech lead with:
- Employer name
- Job title and description
- Required skills
- Whether they accommodate specific barriers
- Hourly wage (if known)

### Reporting Issues
- AI says something strange or inappropriate → screenshot + email tech lead
- Matches seem consistently wrong → note the pattern + email tech lead
- App won't load → check your internet, then email tech lead
